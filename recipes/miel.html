Miel

Description

Coffee recipe that originated from Spain. Traditionally a cortado with honey and cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • Espresso beans
  • Whole milk
  • Honey
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Prepare and pull espresso shots
  2. Steam milk
  3. Stir in honey and cinnamon to preference in espresso
  4. Pour milk
  5. Serve and enjoy
