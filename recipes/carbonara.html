Carbonara

Description

This is an American take on a classic italian pasta recipe made with noodles, egg, spam, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Pasta
  • Parmesan cheese (shredded)

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Cook pasta
  3. Cook spam with onions
  4. Make egg sauce
  5. Mix in all ingredients
  6. Serve
